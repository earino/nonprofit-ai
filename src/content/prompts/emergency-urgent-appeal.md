## The Prompt

```
Write an urgent fundraising appeal for [ORGANIZATION NAME] responding to [EMERGENCY/CRISIS SITUATION].

The appeal should:
- Clearly explain the urgent situation and immediate need
- Specify what funds will be used for: [SPECIFIC USE OF FUNDS]
- Create appropriate urgency without being alarmist
- Include fundraising goal: [GOAL AMOUNT]
- Explain timeline/deadline: [DEADLINE]
- Show how quickly donors' gifts will be deployed
- Be 250-350 words
- Use an [URGENT/SERIOUS/COMPELLING] tone
- Be signed by [SIGNER NAME and TITLE]
```
