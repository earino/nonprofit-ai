## The Prompt

```
Write a 3-email sequence for a year-end giving campaign for [ORGANIZATION NAME].

Email 1 (Early December): Introduce campaign goal of [GOAL AMOUNT], explain need
Email 2 (Mid-December): Share progress update, create urgency
Email 3 (Dec 30-31): Final push, tax-deduction deadline, thank donors

Each email should:
- Be 150-250 words
- Include clear donate link
- Build momentum across the series
- Use [URGENT/HOPEFUL/INSPIRING] tone
- Be signed by [SIGNER NAME and TITLE]
```
