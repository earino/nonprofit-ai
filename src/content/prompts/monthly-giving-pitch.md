## The Prompt

```
Write a persuasive pitch for why donors should give monthly to [ORGANIZATION NAME].

The pitch should:
- Explain benefits of monthly giving for the organization
- Show impact of consistent support: [$AMOUNT/month = ANNUAL IMPACT]
- Address common objections (can cancel anytime, easy to manage)
- Create sense of joining special community ("Sustaining Circle", etc.)
- Suggest starter amounts: [$LOW, $MEDIUM, $HIGH] per month
- Be 200-300 words
- Use an [ENTHUSIASTIC/WARM/CONVINCING] tone
- Include clear call-to-action
```
