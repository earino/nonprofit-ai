## The Prompt

```
Write a one-page executive summary for a grant proposal to [FUNDER NAME] for [ORGANIZATION NAME].

The summary should:
- Hook reader in first sentence with impact or need
- State funding request: [REQUEST AMOUNT] for [PROJECT/PROGRAM NAME]
- Summarize problem/need in 2-3 sentences
- Describe proposed solution/program briefly
- Highlight key outcomes and impact metrics
- Establish organizational credibility
- Be 250-400 words (one page)
- Use [PROFESSIONAL/COMPELLING/CONCISE] tone
```
